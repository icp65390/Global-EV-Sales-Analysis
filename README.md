#  Global Electric Vehicle (EV) Sales Analysis & Forecast (2015–2035)

 
**Tools:** Python, NumPy, Pandas, MySQL, Matplotlib, Jupyter Notebook

---

##  Project Overview
Electric Vehicles (EVs) are rapidly transforming the global automobile industry.  
This real-life project analyzes **global EV sales data for the last 10 years** and predicts **future sales growth for the next decade**.

The project demonstrates **end-to-end data analysis**, from data collection and cleaning to visualization and forecasting.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze historical EV sales growth worldwide
- Identify key growth trends
- Store and manage data using MySQL
- Forecast EV sales for 2025–2035
- Provide business insights to increase EV adoption

- Year
- EV Units Sold (Millions)

Data is based on publicly available global EV market statistics.

---

##  Key Insights
- ![Global-EV-Sales-Analysis Chart](Charts/ev_sales_2015_2025.jpeg)
- EV sales increased from **0.52 million in 2015** to **17.5 million in 2024**
- Growth accelerated after 2020 due to policy incentives and cost reduction
- China dominates global EV sales
- EV adoption is expected to grow exponentially till 2035

---

##  Forecast Summary

- 2030: ~40 million EVs
- 2035: ~70 million EVs
